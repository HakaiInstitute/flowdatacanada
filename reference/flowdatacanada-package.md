# flowdatacanada: Provide access to spatially dispersed river flow datasets in Canada

Provide access to spatially dispersed river flow datasets in Canada
through public APIs and data portals. This package simplifies the
process of retrieving, cleaning, and analyzing river flow data for
hydrological studies and environmental monitoring.
